# Decentralized Network

## Purpose

Describe the later-phase idea of node-to-node collaboration without presenting it as a current Phase 1 deliverable.

In the long term, multiple personal nodes may exchange summaries, models, predictions, or other derived knowledge in privacy-preserving ways.

## Future Direction

Possible collaboration domains include:

- local producers sharing demand signals
- communities coordinating resources
- peers exchanging successful workflows
- groups learning from aggregated patterns

## Relation to Phase 1

Phase 1 should preserve the option for decentralized collaboration, but it should not depend on network protocols or multi-node behavior to justify the initial architecture.
